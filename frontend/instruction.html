How to Play the Learnify's MCQ Game

Welcome to Learnify! Your goal is to answer multiple-choice questions correctly and use the clues you gather to solve the final mystery.

1. Answer the Questions
- On the left, you will see the question.
- On the right, you will see answer choices.
- Select the correct answer to move forward.

2. Navigating the Game
- Go Back Button (Left): Use this to return to the previous question if needed.
- New Button (Right): Move to the next question after answering correctly.
- Clue Button (Middle): Click this to get extra hints and context about the mystery.

3. Solve the Final Mystery
- Once you've answered all the questions correctly, you will unlock the final question.
- Use the clues you've gathered to solve the mystery!
